Our Story

Founded in 2015, Green Haven began as a small group of nature enthusiasts who wanted to make a difference. What started as weekend hiking trips evolved into a movement dedicated to environmental education and sustainable living.

Today, we operate eco-retreats, coordinate conservation projects, and provide educational programs that inspire thousands each year to develop a deeper connection with nature.
